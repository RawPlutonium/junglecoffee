ABOUT US

Jungle estate coffee ltd is a farm specialized in the production of very high quality premium specialty coffee. It’s owned by Keremara ltd, an umbrella company that mothers other diverse companies ranging from agricultural activities, manufacturing foods and beverages, hotel and tourism to the larger real estate business.

The estate is named Jungle because it is within a dense indigenous bush land. It’s located between two permanent rivers at 1740 meters above sea level on deep volcanic soils that contribute to the superior flavors of Jungle coffee(s). The passion to ensure a great cup of coffee each season is borne by the estate in the production cycle through harmonized management practices leaving each coffee tree perfectly crafted to take advantage of the beautiful natural environment thus delivering an excellent sip each time. The result of this is definitely a deeper relationship between you and our superior flavors of coffee blends built to create a story of freedom.

Your great coffee source( Jungle estate coffee) is sandwiched between the Aberdare mountain range (3990m) and Mt. Kenya(5200m) both seen from various points on the estate.



A beautiful climate, legendary views, Jungle forests, rich soils, amazing bird life and most importantly, our people are truly God given assets that are the source of our success. Our story spans from the early 1900’s in the colonial era through the Mau Mau wars for freedom from the colonialists into independence. Chania river and River Muringato whose source is the crystal dew drops from the pristine and misty moorlands of the beautiful mountains create beautiful borders for the estate.

Why we do what we do

Coffee production Jungle coffee recognizes the need for quality production of coffee thus perfecting the art from the farm to the cup. Our premium Arabica coffees form the world’s most dense, striking, well balanced acidic tasting coffees with fruity black berry(currant) notes. Our annual production of top highland grades for the unit stands at approximately 80tonnes with an expected outlook of 150tons in the coming 2-5 years. Jungle’s AA quality class 3 matures over a period of 9months with flowering starting around the first week of March. Our coffees are carefully processed for your liberating consumption in the estate and managed through professional management services.


Exporting Coffee Over the years, our export markets have grown considerably and our export coffees have become a demand for coffee connoisseurs all over the world. This is greatly attributed to our sustainable coffee growing practices which are environment and social friendly thus producing great flavors that are consumer friendly and that give the story of freedom from the source. Jungle estate Coffees are sold at the Nairobi Coffee Exchange between the months of January and May each year under the estate brand name. We also sell our coffee through our marketing agents and through our marketing Communications office under the Jungle Estate coffee brand name. Certification by various coffee bodies nationally and internationally also contributes largely to our growing coffee markets due to the uniqueness of the brand our people are passionately creating just to satisfy and sooth your diverse taste buds.


Local markets At Jungle coffee, we carry the belief that we cannot satisfy our guests before our children are fed lest….you know. Therefore, we are working harder to provide coffee products and coffee based products for the consumption of Kenya and our region in particular. The Kenyan people share with us a story of freedom from the colonial times because we fought together to have what we have today. It’s to this end therefore that we enjoy having our fellow countrymen and women enjoying this beautiful story of freedom inform of great cups of coffee. Our coffee products are available in Supermarkets countrywide, hotels and other distribution points. Our packs sell themselves to you and when you remember the ‘story of freedom’ in the packages, you can’t avoid this superior high quality Arabica coffee which is unlike the usual caffeine full instantly soluble coffee. Our coffee has been rated characteristically classy, purposefully pure by various connoisseurs and this is inspired by our heritage and the shujaas in our motherland Kenya.


Trainings Whereas we may not be an outsourcing company from the large out growers within our region due to regularity and control issues, Jungle estate coffee is actively participating in training local farmers in the region and school going students and pupils in Sustainable coffee production practices. The company has taken the role in the region to build sustainable coffee families, coffee trees, coffee practices, coffee communities and this is a focus beyond the cup. To this end, not only does Jungle coffee get the support from the community and stakeholders around but the small scale farmers get maximum output from their coffee and the region gets more acclaimed for high quality premium Arabicas.


Agricultural diversity Jungle estate does more than coffee. To maintain the name and grow in standards as an all round estate catering for various food needs, Jungle estate has a dairy farm with a milk production capacity of 100litres/day which is supplied to regional learning institutions and the community at a highly subsidized rate. Jungle estate also maintains a large pig farm for pork production which is supplied to the local schools on demand and also sold to pork processing companies in Kenya. Jungle estate is also involved in horticultural farming that is very vast and has diversified vegetables during different seasons in the year. These are bought by our employees at a subsidized rate in the aim of promoting healthy eating habits within our resourceful community. The vegetables also cater for the demands of our tourism and hospitality sector of the overall Keremara holdings limited.
